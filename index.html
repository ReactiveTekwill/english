Your Life, Your Habits — A Quick Guide to a Healthier Mind & Body

School. Exams. Group chats. Expectations. Here’s the short version: tiny smart habits can boost energy, focus, and mood—without a total life makeover.

Why it matters

🎯 Better Brain, Better Days

Healthy habits = stronger focus, steadier mood, faster recovery from stress. Think of them as upgrades for learning, memory, and confidence.

Foundations

🤝 Tip 1 — Social Connections

  • ✔Weekly check-in with a friend (walk & talk).
  • ✔Join a club / study group—share the load.
  • ✔Ask for help early; offer help often.
Calm Tools

🧘 Tip 2 — Stress, Handled

Three micro-habits you can start today:

50/10 focus cycles 2-min stretch Hobby flow (music, art, code)
Non-negotiable

🌙 Tip 3 — Sleep Fuels Grades

Target ~8 hours most nights.

  • ✔Same bedtime & wake time.
  • ✔Screens off 60 min before bed.
  • ✔Phone parked across the room.
Daily Energy

🥗🏃 Tip 4 — Food & Movement, Simplified

Build most meals like this:

🚀 One Tiny Step This Week

Text a friend to plan a walk, try a 3-minute breathing reset before homework, set a realistic bedtime, or add one green thing to your plate. Small wins compound.
